Historical Development

A concise timeline of key milestones that shaped modern AI.

1950
Turing Test — Alan Turing proposes a test for machine intelligence.
1956
Dartmouth Workshop — The birth of AI as an academic discipline.
1997
Deep Blue defeats world chess champion Garry Kasparov.
2012
Deep Learning Breakthroughs — Image recognition improvements spark renewed investment.
2020s
Large Language Models and foundation models enter mainstream use in products and research.
