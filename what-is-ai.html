What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the field of computer science focused on building systems that perform tasks that typically require human intelligence — learning, reasoning, perception, and language understanding.

Branches

  • Machine Learning (ML)
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Computer Vision
  • Robotics

Techniques

  • Supervised Learning
  • Unsupervised Learning
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Deep Learning

Characteristics

  • Adaptation
  • Generalization
  • Automation & Optimization

Common Use Cases

  1. Recommendation systems (e.g., streaming & e-commerce)
  2. Conversational agents and customer support
  3. Medical imaging & diagnostics
  4. Autonomous vehicles and robotics
